What we know so far about Moctos? Moctos, an octopus-like monster that lives in the swamp surrounded by luminous beetles. He loves bubbles and you will find bubbles all around the swamp. He would spend his free time chasing bubbles around the swamp. His mother taught him how to create bubbles using his ink when he was younger. To this day he still finds it fun and it brings him a lot of joy. If you click one, he will happily pop it for you.But be careful! Moctos hates birds. Moctos was once an outgoing and curious young creature who loved exploring. Then one day he approached a predatory bird's nest too close while exploring and one of the birds blinded him in one eye. Since then he lived with his fear and hatred of birds. This resulted not only in a change in his personality but also led him to develop ink-shooting skills to keep himself safe. So if you press on the bird he will react by shooting ink everywhere which makes your screen black.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature You are a scientist(Loa Kivin) who is watching the viral tiktok video which was taken by the teenagers. If you press on the bubbles, moctos will go to the bubble and pop it for you. But if you press on the bird, he will shoot ink everywhere to protect himself(he is scared of birds) which will make your phone screen go black.
